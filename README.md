CursedWikipedia

Wikipedia for things that don't exist. The perfect response to "Source?".
Type in anything. A vague concept, a made-up creature, a feeling you can't name... 
CursedWikipedia writes a full Wikipedia-style article about it. Infobox, citations, controversy section, the works. All completely fake, all completely straight-faced.

What it does:
  Generates a full article for any concept you throw at it
  Wikilinks to related fictional concepts
  Tracks your session history so you can revisit anything you've generated
  Includes a random article button if you're feeling brave

How to use it:
  Download cursed_wikipedia.html and open it in your browser.
